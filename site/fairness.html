Provably Fair,
not “trust us.”

Before mint we publish a commitment hash. After mint we reveal the salt. Anyone can recompute every chest. If the hashes match, the roll was fair.

Plain language

  1. Lock the rules. Traits, weights, tiers, and the RNG algorithm are hashed into one commitment before anyone pays.
  2. Pay with a coin. Your payment coin id becomes the seed for your chest — not a server roll you can’t check.
  3. Reveal the salt. After mint closes, the secret salt is published. Re-run the same math at home.
  4. Verify. chest_roller.py verify must reproduce the published manifest bit-for-bit.
