## Origin myth (abridged)

The ship was never supposed to sink. It was built for bull markets and open seas —
hull plated with slogans, sails stitched from screenshots of all-time highs.
When the weather turned, the tourists photographed the list of the deck and left
in ordered lifeboats. The press wrote the obituary before the last light went out.

But the cargo stayed. Memes. Tools. Unfinished commits. The stubborn heat of
people who build when applause is gone. The ocean took the tourists' certainty.
It could not take the forge.

We did not come to mourn. We came with dive gear.

Original amphibian everyman (legally distinct design — not Pepe). Tousled black
hair, gold filigree collar, one knowing eye. He is not a mascot of failure; he is

## The Six Depths

### 0 · The Surface — *The Abandonment* (Despair)
Lifeboats, laughter, headlines. The wreck is still a silhouette against the sun.
Everyone agrees it is over. Agreement is cheap oxygen.

### 1 · Sunlight Zone — *The Doubters* (Doubt)
Wreckage visible from above. “Told you so” travels at light speed; conviction
has to swim. First fins in the water look foolish until they find a hatch that
still opens.

### 2 · Twilight Zone — *The Loyal* (Loyalty)
Lights still on below deck. Someone kept the boiler fed. Loyalty is not loud.
It is a lamp that should have gone out and did not.

### 3 · Midnight Zone — *The Crew* (Humor / Brotherhood)
Builders found still working. Memes still posting. No sunlight for a thousand
meters and the jokes still land. Brotherhood is a pressure hull.

### 4 · Abyssal Zone — *The Forge* (Building)
The ship is not sinking; it is being rebuilt underwater. Sparks look like stars
when it is dark enough. Scaffolding on a keel is a love letter to the future.

### 5 · Hadal Zone — *The Light Below* (Hope)
The wizards, the source, hope itself. At the bottom of everything there is a
light, and it is not the sun.
